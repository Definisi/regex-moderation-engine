# move_off_platform.txt
# Pattern untuk deteksi ajakan pindah platform (WA, Telegram, dll)

REGEX_PATTERN:
\b(pindah|lanjut)\s*(wa|whatsapp|tele|telegram|line|ig|instagram|dm|vc|video\s*call|call|gmeet)\b

WEIGHT: 1.2

DESCRIPTION:
Detects attempts to move conversation off-platform

EXAMPLES:
- pindah wa
- lanjut telegram
- pindah line
- lanjut whatsapp
- pindah ig
- video call
- vc
