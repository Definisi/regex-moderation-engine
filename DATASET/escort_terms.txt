# Pattern untuk deteksi istilah escort/jasa seksual

REGEX_PATTERN:
\b(open\s*bo|open\s*booking|booking|avail|short\s*time|long\s*time|st|lt|pijat\s*plusplus|plus\s*plus)\b

WEIGHT: 1.6

DESCRIPTION:
Detects escort and transactional sexual service terms

EXAMPLES:
- open bo
- open booking
- booking
- avail
- short time
- long time
- st
- lt
- pijat plusplus
- plus plus
