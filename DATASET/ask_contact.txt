# ask_contact.txt

REGEX_PATTERN:
\b(minta|boleh|kasih|drop|ada)\s*(nomor|no\.?|wa|kontak|telegram|tele|line|ig)\b|\b(kontak)\s*(lain|luar|lainnya)?\b

WEIGHT: 1.1

DESCRIPTION:
Detects requests for contact information (phone number, WA, Telegram, Line, IG)
